Wrestle Bros Space is a browser game discovery site built around curated landing pages, category navigation, and clear support and policy information.

What The Site Covers

Wrestle Bros Space focuses on browser-based games that are easy to launch, easy to understand, and suitable for short repeat sessions. The catalog is organized into action, battle royale, FPS, multiplayer, and sniper categories, with Wrestle Bros serving as the featured homepage title.

Why We Use Landing Pages

Each game is presented inside its own landing page instead of on a blank embed-only screen. That layout gives visitors context before they click play: a short description, category placement, support links, legal pages, and navigation to related titles.

What We Try To Make Clear

  • What type of player a game is best for.
  • Whether the game is more competitive, casual, tactical, or progression-driven.
  • How the page fits into the broader catalog.
  • Where visitors can go for privacy, support, or copyright concerns.

What The Site Does Not Claim

Wrestle Bros Space does not claim ownership of every embedded game itself. Some titles rely on third-party providers. Our role is to organize, describe, and present these pages with clear structure and site-level support information.

How We Keep The Site Usable

  • We maintain a root-level ads.txt file and crawlable sitemap.
  • We keep privacy, cookie, terms, DMCA, and contact pages publicly available.
  • We link important support and legal pages from core site areas.
  • We update landing-page wording to reduce template repetition and improve clarity.
