This page answers common questions about how Wrestle Bros Space organizes game pages, embeds, support requests, and advertising readiness.

Is Wrestle Bros Space a game studio?

No. Wrestle Bros Space is a browser game discovery and landing-page site. Some pages include third-party embedded games, while the site itself provides categorization, descriptions, and support information.

Why does each game have its own page?

Dedicated landing pages make browsing easier. They allow each title to include a clear title, description, category placement, related links, and access to policy or support pages.

Do I need to register to use the site?

No. Wrestle Bros Space does not require a site account to browse categories or launch game pages.

Are all games made by the same publisher?

No. Some game pages rely on third-party providers. Wrestle Bros Space does not claim ownership of every embedded title and provides separate legal and contact pages for support or rights issues.

Why are there site-wide policy pages?

Those pages explain privacy, cookies, support, terms, and copyright handling. They help visitors understand how the site works beyond the playable content.

Will ads appear on every page?

Ad serving depends on review, approval, and policy enforcement by Google. The site is structured to support AdSense, but ads are not guaranteed on every page or at every time.

How do I report a broken page or incorrect description?

Use the contact page and include the exact page URL plus a short explanation of the issue.

How are games reviewed for the site?

See How We Review Games and Editorial Standards for more detail about page quality, metadata, categorization, and content updates.
