Overview

Wrestle Bros Space is a browser game discovery and play site. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use the site, how that information is used, and how you can contact us about privacy questions.

Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, Wrestle Bros Space may collect basic technical data such as IP address, browser type, device information, referring pages, and approximate usage timing through server logs, analytics tools, and embedded services.

Analytics And Advertising

We use Google Analytics to understand traffic and site performance. We also prepare pages for Google AdSense. These services may collect or process information according to their own policies, including the use of cookies or similar technologies.

Embedded Games And Third-Party Services

Some pages on this site include embedded game content delivered by third-party providers. When you load or interact with those pages, the provider may receive technical information such as your IP address, browser data, and referrer information.

Site Contact

If you email us, we receive the contact information and message content you choose to send. We use that information only to respond to your request, investigate issues, or handle legal notices.

Cookies

Cookies may be used by analytics, advertising partners, and embedded content providers to support measurement, fraud prevention, preferences, and ad delivery. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

How We Use Information

  • Operate and improve the site experience.
  • Understand traffic patterns and page performance.
  • Respond to support and legal requests.
  • Prevent abuse, fraud, or technical misuse.

Children's Privacy

Wrestle Bros Space is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support email or another direct interaction, contact us and we will review the request.

Your Choices

You can manage cookies in your browser, avoid optional third-party services by leaving the site, and contact us if you want us to review or delete information you submitted directly by email.
