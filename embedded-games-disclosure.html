Wrestle Bros Space combines original editorial landing-page content with playable third-party browser game embeds on selected pages.

What This Site Publishes

Wrestle Bros Space is not a game studio. It is a browser game curation site that organizes selected titles into readable landing pages. Those pages are designed to help visitors understand what kind of game they are opening before the playable content loads.

Where Embedded Games Come From

Some playable titles on this site are delivered through third-party browser game providers. When an embed appears on a page, Wrestle Bros Space is presenting that playable experience inside its own editorial wrapper rather than claiming authorship of the underlying game itself.

What Original Value We Add

  • Category organization across action, battle royale, FPS, multiplayer, and sniper sections.
  • Page-specific summaries that explain tone, pace, audience fit, and quick browser tips.
  • Site-level support, privacy, copyright, and contact paths that are reachable from core pages.
  • A reviewed featured selection rather than a blank embed directory.

Why Some Older Pages Are Less Visible

Not every older page remains in the actively featured catalog at all times. Pages with thinner descriptions, weaker categorization, or lower editorial value may stay accessible by direct URL while being removed from featured navigation until they are revised.

How Rights And Accuracy Issues Are Handled

If you believe a page description is inaccurate, an embed should not be displayed, or a rights concern needs review, contact us through the contact page or the DMCA page. We use those reports to revisit page visibility, wording, or removal decisions.
